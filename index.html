Oregon Trail'R Teardrop Trailers

finest-quality, beautiful teardrop campers

FronTear is our Flagship model traditional teardrop camper. Beautiful classic styling looks great behind any tow vehicle. FronTear is lightweight and can easily be towed behind most cars. FronTear model teardrops start at $14000. They are fully configurable with many options available on an ala-carte basis. FronTear can be configured to suit your budget and needs.
TerraDrop is our edgy, tough, off-roading offering. Designed to compliment the styling cues of today's off road capable vehicles, TerraDrop can be configured to be VERY capable for rough terrain, and overland travel. TerraDrop Starts at $16,000. They are fully configurable with many options available on an ala-carte basis. TerraDrop can be configured to suit your budget and needs.
Do-Drop is small and simple, but totally ready for adventure! This sporty teardrop can be configured in either 3 1/2 ft wide or 4 ft wide versions. The 3 1/2 ft version is ideal to tow behind side-by-side utility vehicles, like Yamaha Rhinos and Arctic cat units. Do-Drop can be hauled out into the woods on narrow roads and trails. Ideal for sportsman / fishermen / adventure seekers. The 3 1/2 ft version is ideal for a single occupant, but could possibly work for two, given its' bed which is a little larger than twin size. The 4 ft version is still small, but more suited to dual occupancy.

DO-Drop pricing starts at $6150, and is configurable with a small number of highly effective upgrades/options.
